Space Questions & Answers

❓ How far is the Moon from Earth?

The average distance is about 384,400 kilometers. Light from the Moon takes about 1.3 seconds to reach Earth.

❓ Why do astronauts float in space?

Astronauts appear to float because they are in continuous free fall while orbiting Earth, creating microgravity conditions that is totally difference from outer space.

❓ What is the International Space Station?

The ISS is a laboratory orbiting Earth where astronauts conduct scientific research and technology experiments.

❓ How hot is the Sun?

The Sun's surface temperature is approximately 5,500°C while its core reaches around 15 million°C.
